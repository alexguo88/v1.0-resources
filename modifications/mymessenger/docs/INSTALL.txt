MyMessenger UseBB Install Instructions
------------------------------------------

1. MAKE BACKUPS!
2. Run "sql/mymessenger.sql" in PHPMyAdmin to alter your database.
3. Upload "upload/panel_messenger.php" to the "/sources" directory of your UseBB Installation.
4. Upload "upload/page_head.php" to the "/sources" directory of your UseBB Installation.
5. Upload "upload/panel.php" to the root directory of your UseBB Installation, overwriting the stock copy.
6. Upload "upload/profile.php" to the root directory of your UseBB Installation, overwriting the stock copy.

You're Done!
